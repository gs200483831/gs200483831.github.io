ZOO MELAKA, ESTABLISHED SINCE 1963 UNDER THE CARE OF MELAKA STATE GOVERNMENT HAS RECENTLY BEEN PRIVATIZED IN THE EARLY 2013 UNDER THE NEW NAME OF MELAKA ZOO AND NIGHT SAFARI. This 54 acres second largest zoo in Malaysia under the new Majlis Perbandaran Hang Tuah Jaya will see a transformation of the zoo to an open concept in animal exhibit, enhancing wildlife education programme, animal breeding and conservation programme and making the zoo to be a recreational area. The ever popular Night Safari will be enhanced to give visitors a unique opportunity in observing the behaviour of nocturnal animals in their natural habitats. At Melaka Zoo & Night Safari, it is not just about a walk through our animals but a whole new wildlife discovery with our various programs suitable from pre-schoolers to university students and corporate companies.
This rugged outdoor activity is for the wild challengers not only for the university and college students but also for corporate companies seeking extreme fun in the zoo! Mind you, Zoomanji program is also an ideal team building gateway where communication and teamwork will be put to test. We welcome you to Zoomanji for massive adrenaline challenges! For more details on our Zoomanji program please contact our Education Department.
